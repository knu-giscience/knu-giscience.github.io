Developing a spatial multi-dimensional mobility index (MDMI) and producing
nationwide maps of mobility patterns for policy use.

**Sponsor**: 한국교통연구원 (KOTI)
**Period**: 2025.08 – 2026.02

The project extends the lab's MDMI methodology to cover all administrative
districts of South Korea, providing visual and quantitative diagnostics of
regional mobility.
