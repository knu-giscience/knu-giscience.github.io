**GIScience & Spatial Bigdata Lab** at the Department of Geography,
Kyungpook National University, studies how geographic information science
and spatial big data can answer real-world questions about cities,
regions, and people.

Our research spans three pillars:

- **Geographic Information Science (GIScience)** — representing and
  analyzing the real world geographically, with an eye on the digital-twin
  era.
- **Spatial Big Data** — discovering hidden spatial patterns from mobile,
  social-media, drone, and other location-aware data sources.
- **Mobility & Future Vehicles** — understanding everyday human mobility
  and the spatial implications of electric, autonomous, and shared
  vehicles.

We work closely with the Korea Research Institute for Human Settlements
(KRIHS), the Korea Transport Institute (KOTI), and international partners
including UC Davis and UC Santa Barbara.
